Course Name: DevOps for System Administration
Course Code: COMP 2156
Prof name: Pritesh Patel

The course is offered to students in the T147, T141, T187, and T191 programs.
It is a 2nd year course and has a pre-requisite, which is COMP 2155.

The course is designed to teach students all the skills and knowledge needed for a DevOps engineer.

The course teaches students how to use GitHub, and students will learn about various data formats and types, such as XML, JSON, YAML, and YANG.
